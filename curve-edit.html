curve-edit -- tool to generate linear curves for POV-Ray
curve-edit requires canvas support.
POV-Ray list:
Usage:
  • Double-click to add a point.
  • Drag a point to move it.
  • Lathe curves in POV-Ray will be revolved around the right side of the view.
